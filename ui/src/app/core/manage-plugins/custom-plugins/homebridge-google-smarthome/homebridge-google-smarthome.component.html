Homebridge Google Smart Home

The Homebridge Google Smart Home plugin allows you to control your Homebridge accessories from a Google Home enabled smart speaker or the Google Home mobile app.

To enable this functionality you must sign in with your Google or GitHub account; make sure you use the same account when adding the Homebridge action in the Google Home mobile app.
